SmartexLab is a full-service software development company specializing in both web and mobile software.

We deliver quality digital products that help transform small and mid-size businesses

Since 2014, we have been applying the cutting edge of technology and our expert knowledge across multiple domains. SmartexLab’s core focus is software development for fintech and healthcare. We also provide comprehensive services for retail, travel, media and entertainment to help small and mid-size businesses reach their strategic goals by developing effective and high-quality software products.

How We Work

Be transparent

Trust enables both sides to see our progress and allows for effective communication 24/7.

Be proactive

We are not just a vendor. Our clients are our partners in creating value. We dive into the specifics of your business, assist in choosing the right technology and guide through the development cycle to achieve success.

Be flexible

We follow an agile methodology that allows us to quickly adapt to any challenges and rapidly changing business needs.

Be result-driven

When we commit to something, we define a specific, measurable, and realistic goal you want to achieve and deliver on results, meeting the deadlines and budgets.
